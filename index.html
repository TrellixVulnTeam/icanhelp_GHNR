About Us

Hello there!

IF you appreciate a random act of kindness to or from a stranger,
IF you know that nothing feels better or more rewarding than a selfless deed
or IF you know how it feels to be grateful to someone's hand when in need,

you are in the right place!

We are a free yet a very expensive service, its capital is the goodness that resides within everyone of us.
Our profit is the most rewarding feelings of a selfless act of kindness to a stragner or a genuine gratitude to that stranger that lent a hand.
You can volunteer to take or deliver the grocery for an elderly neighbour, you can help a nieghbour with their garden if you have the skill, as well you could volunteer to take some neighbours' dogs on a walk and enjoy the company of beautiful souls, or you could help a neighbour with a move or simple repair work if you have the will and skill.
The rules are simple: treat people fairly the way you wish to be treated, if you offer help or request help and you committed to it then see it through - it is all about free well and commitment, and last "and always" Be kind!.
We do not live in the peaceful or comfortable world we wish we could, but with these little bits of random acts of kindness we can surely make it a kinder place, even one day at a time.
